Pathanamthitta is the Pilgrim Capital of Kerala, where spirituality, nature and culture intertwine. Explore Sabarimala Temple, Aranmula Boat Race, vibrant Padayani rituals, and eco-tourism hotspots like Gavi and Adavi and immerse in its rich heritage, lush forests, and serene landscapes of Pathanamthitta.

Apart from its spiritual aura, Pathanamthitta is blessed with picturesque landscapes adorned with serene rivers, lush green hills, and mesmerizing waterfalls. It offers eco-tourism spots like Gavi and the enchanting Perunthenaruvi Waterfall for a serene escape, while the Konni Elephant Training Center adds a touch of wildlife wonder. The district is also rich in culture and craftsmanship, showcasing traditional handicrafts like wood, coconut shell, and brassware. Among them, the rare and exclusive metal mirror, Aranmula Kannadi, is a true masterpiece. Whether you seek spiritual solace, natural beauty, or a blend of both, Pathanamthitta welcomes visitors with open arms, inviting them to explore its religious heritage and scenic landscapes.
