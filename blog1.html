My Morning Routine

March 30, 2025

Today I woke up at 7:00 AM and started my morning with a cup of coffee. It's been a little chilly lately, so I made sure to put on my warm jacket and take a brisk walk around the neighborhood. I enjoy these quiet moments to think and plan my day.
